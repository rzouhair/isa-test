Pass your ISA Certified Arborist exam on the first try. Hundreds of real practice questions, full mock exams, and complete coverage of every ISA exam domain.

Whether you're studying for the International Society of Arboriculture's Certified Arborist credential, this app gives you everything you need: drill-down practice, instant explanations, and progress tracking that shows where you stand on exam day.

WHAT'S INSIDE

• Hundreds of practice questions across all ISA Certified Arborist exam domains
• Full-length mock exams matching the official pass threshold
• Flashcards mode — quick review of key concepts and terminology
• Learn Mode — drill by topic with instant feedback
• Review Mode — every missed question with a full explanation
• Weak Questions — auto-surfaces what you keep getting wrong
• Bookmarks — save tough questions to revisit
• Stats dashboard — accuracy by topic, passing ratio, weak-spot count
• Exam date countdown — set your target date, get reminders

ISA EXAM DOMAINS COVERED

• Soil Science — soil composition, moisture, fertility, drainage
• Tree Biology — anatomy, physiology, growth, structure
• Tree Identification — species, characteristics, hardiness
• Plant Health Care — pest management, disease diagnosis, treatment
• Pruning — techniques, timing, cut placement, tools
• Tree Installation — site selection, planting, transplanting
• Tree Support Systems — cabling, bracing, propping
• Climbing & Work Practices — gear, knots, safety
• Tree Risk — assessment basics, hazard identification
• Tree Protection — construction site protection, root zones
• Diagnosis & Treatment — common disorders, preventive care
• Urban Forestry — community trees, ordinances, ecosystem services
