# App Store Screenshot Plan — CDL Prep: Practice Test 2026

Strategy: outrank Khai Vuong (purple), Hanh Le (navy), and CDL Genie/Elegant eLearning (bright blue + amber — market leader w/ 180K ratings). Our palette is now aligned to the same CDL-blue space + amber (`theme.accent = #2563EB`, `theme.accentWarm = #F59E0B`).

## Positioning rules

1. **First three screenshots drive 80% of downloads.** Slot 1–3 must answer: "pass my CDL?", "real questions?", "covers my endorsement?"
2. **Big bold amber headline on solid blue bg** — the CDL Genie formula that's already winning the chart. Match and differentiate.
3. **Never claim what we don't have.** No "450k drivers", no "FMCSA-licensed ELDT", no "99.06% success rate", no "4K video".
4. **Substitute with what we DO have**: 1,020+ real questions, 9 CDL categories, all endorsements, timed exam simulator @ 80%, Duolingo-style level path, weak-question review, exam countdown, per-category stats donut, cheat sheets.
5. **Keyword anchoring per slot** — pull from the validated list (`cdl`, `cdl prep`, `cdl test prep`, `cdl practice test`, `cdl hazmat practice test`, `cdl prep test 2026`, `dmv practice test 2026`, `hazmat`, `inspection testing`, `free cdl practice test`, `theory test`, `pass`, `permit driving log`).

## Shared design spec (apply to every slot)

- **Canvas**: 1290×2796 (iPhone 6.9" Display — App Store required), safe-area 96px top/bottom
- **Background**: solid `#2563EB` OR subtle radial gradient `#2563EB → #1E40AF` (diagonal)
- **Headline**: ~130pt, **Nunito Black** or **SF Pro Rounded Black**, amber `#FBBF24` for power word, white for rest, letter-spacing −2%
- **Subtitle**: ~56pt, SF Pro Rounded Medium, white 75% opacity
- **Pill badge under subtitle** (optional): capsule, white border 1.5pt, 12pt vertical padding, amber text
- **Device frame**: iPhone 15 Pro frame (titanium), 18pt outline
- **Shadow**: `rgba(0,0,0,0.35)` blur 48, y 24 — elevates phone off blue
- **Text-to-device ratio**: 40/60 (text top, phone bottom) — matches CDL Genie's winning pattern
