# Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome! Here you'll find answers to the most common questions about the **ISA Arborist Exam Prep** app.

---

## 📚 App Features & Functionality

**1. What exam does the app prepare me for?**

ISA Arborist Exam Prep covers the **International Society of Arboriculture (ISA) Certified Arborist** credential exam — the entry-level professional credential for arborists in North America.

**2. Which ISA exam domains are included?**

All 10 official ISA Certified Arborist exam domains:

- Tree Biology
- Identification & Selection
- Soil Management
- Installation & Establishment
- Pruning
- Diagnosis & Treatment
- Tree Protection
- Tree Risk Management
- Safe Work Practices
- Urban Forestry

**3. How many questions are included?**

750 practice questions across the 10 exam domains, plus 250 flashcards covering key concepts and terminology.

**4. Are the questions identical to the real ISA exam?**

No. ISA exam questions are confidential. The Application mirrors the format, difficulty, and topic distribution so you can study what actually matters and pass on the first try.

**5. Does the app cover specialty ISA credentials (TRAQ, BCMA, Utility, Municipal)?**

No. The Application focuses exclusively on the **ISA Certified Arborist** credential. Specialty credentials are not covered in this version.

**6. Does the app work offline?**

Yes. The full question bank and flashcard library are bundled — no internet needed for practice sessions, mock exams, flashcards, or reviews.

**7. What is Learn Mode?**

Drill by exam domain with instant feedback and explanations so you build mastery one topic at a time.

**8. What is Mock Exam Mode?**

A timed, full-length simulator that mirrors the real ISA Certified Arborist exam — including question count, time limit, and the official 76% pass threshold.

**9. What is Review Mode?**

Every missed question with a full plain-English explanation so you understand *why* the right answer is right.

**10. What are Weak Questions?**

The app auto-tracks questions you keep getting wrong using a spaced-repetition system and resurfaces them so you fix knowledge gaps fast.

**11. What are Flashcards?**

Quick-review cards for key concepts, terminology, anatomy, and definitions. Use the Anki-style 4-button rating (Again / Hard / Good / Easy) to drive a spaced-repetition schedule that focuses your time on weak material.

**12. Can I bookmark tough questions or flashcards?**

Yes — save any question or flashcard to revisit later from the Bookmarks tab.

**13. Is there an exam-date countdown?**

Yes. Set a target ISA exam date during onboarding and get reminders at 30, 14, 7, 3, and 1 day out.

**14. Can I see my progress?**

The Stats dashboard shows accuracy by exam domain, current pass-rate trend, weak-spot count, and study streak.

## 🚀 Getting Started

**15. How do I start practicing?**

Open the app, set your target ISA exam date, then choose any topic to begin. No sign-up required.

**16. Do I need to create an account?**

No. Progress saves locally on your device.

**17. How should I study to pass?**

1. Drill each domain in **Learn Mode** until accuracy is >80%
2. Run a full timed **Mock Exam** to gauge readiness against the 76% pass threshold
3. Use **Review Mode** + **Weak Questions** to close gaps
4. Use **Flashcards** daily for terminology and concepts
5. Run a final mock exam the week before your real test

**18. What should I focus on first?**

Tree Biology, Pruning, and Soil Management are foundational and appear most heavily on the exam. Build a solid base there before drilling specialty domains.

**19. How long does it take to prepare?**

Most candidates study 6–12 weeks at 30–60 minutes per day. The app's Stats dashboard helps you self-assess readiness against the pass threshold.

## 🌳 ISA Certification Background

**42. What is the ISA Certified Arborist credential?**

A professional certification awarded by the International Society of Arboriculture to individuals who demonstrate competence in arboriculture through experience, education, and a comprehensive written exam.

**43. What are the eligibility requirements for the real exam?**

Either: 3+ years of full-time arboricultural work experience, **OR** a degree in arboriculture/horticulture/forestry plus reduced experience requirements. See the [official ISA website](https://www.isa-arbor.com/) for current eligibility rules.

**44. What is the passing score?**

The official ISA Certified Arborist exam pass threshold is 76%. The Application's Mock Exam mode mirrors this threshold.

**45. Is this app affiliated with ISA?**

No. The Application is an independent study tool. ISA® and Certified Arborist® are trademarks of the International Society of Arboriculture. The Application is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by ISA.
