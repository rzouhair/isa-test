## 📚 App Features & Functionality

**1. What exam does the app prepare me for?**

CDL Prep covers the **2026 U.S. Commercial Driver's License (CDL) written knowledge test** for Class A, Class B, and all federal endorsements.

**2. Which CDL categories are included?**

All 9 official categories: **General Knowledge, Hazmat, Air Brakes, Combination Vehicles, Passenger Transport, School Bus, Pre-Trip Inspection, Tanker, Doubles & Triples** — 1,025 questions total.

**3. Are the questions up to date for 2026?**

Yes. Questions follow the latest FMCSA-aligned CDL manuals and are reviewed regularly.

**4. Are the questions identical to the real DMV test?**

No. Real exam questions are confidential. CDL Prep mirrors the format, difficulty, and topics so you can pass on the first try.

**5. Does the app cover my state?**

Yes — federal CDL content applies in all 50 states. Pick your state during onboarding to load the right handbook and target pass threshold (default 80%).

**6. Does the app work offline?**

Yes. The full question bank is bundled — no internet needed for practice sessions, exams, or reviews.

**7. What is Learn Mode?**

Drill by category with instant feedback, explanations, and unlockable level progression so you build mastery one topic at a time.

**8. What is Exam Mode?**

A timed simulator that mirrors the real CDL written test, including the 80% pass threshold and category mix.

**9. What is Review Mode?**

Every missed question with a full plain-English explanation so you understand *why* the right answer is right.

**10. What are Weak Questions?**

The app auto-tracks questions you keep getting wrong and resurfaces them so you fix gaps fast.

**11. What are Cheat Sheets?**

Quick-reference summaries (air brake checks, hazmat placards, pre-trip steps, etc.) for last-minute review.

**12. Can I bookmark tough questions?**

Yes — save any question to revisit later from the Bookmarks tab.

**13. Is there an exam-date countdown?**

Yes. Set a target date during onboarding and get reminders at 30, 14, 7, 3, and 1 day out.

**14. Can I see my progress?**

The Stats dashboard shows accuracy by category, current pass rate, weak-spot count, and study streak.

## 🚀 Getting Started

**15. How do I start practicing?**

Open the app, pick your state and target exam date, then choose any category to begin. No sign-up required.

**16. Do I need to create an account?**

No. Progress saves locally on your device.

**17. How should I study to pass?**

1. Drill each category in Learn Mode until accuracy is >85%
2. Run a full timed Exam to gauge readiness
3. Use Review Mode + Weak Questions to close gaps
4. Skim Cheat Sheets the night before

**18. What endorsements should I study?**

Only the ones you need — Hazmat (H), Tanker (N), Doubles/Triples (T), Passenger (P), School Bus (S). General Knowledge and Air Brakes apply to most candidates.

**19. Are official handbooks included?**

Yes — the in-app Handbook viewer hosts official state CDL manuals.
